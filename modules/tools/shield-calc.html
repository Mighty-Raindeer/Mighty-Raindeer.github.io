Shielding Calculator

Shielding Equation:

D = D₀ × (1/r²) × e^(-μx)

Where:

  • D = Dose rate at point of calculation
  • D₀ = Initial dose rate at 1m
  • r = Distance from source (m)
  • μ = Linear attenuation coefficient
  • x = Shield thickness (cm)
